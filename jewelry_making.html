Jewelry Making

The perfect accessory has the power to instantly transform an outfit! It can dress it up (or down), add a pop of color, or even a bit of whimsy. Creating your own jewelry gives you the freedom to create something unique to your own style, for every occassion.

My personal taste in jewelry has always been quite varied. I love adding large, colorful, statement jewelry to an otherwise basic outfit to go from "meh" to "Wow!" or adding a small stud to an already amazing outfit just to polish the look. After years of purchasing my jewelry, I decided to finally put my own abilities to use and create my own!

Here I'll show you just some of the materials and tools I use most often to complete my pieces.
